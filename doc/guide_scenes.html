Quick Start Guide

Guide to Scenes

Header / Live Statistics

Shows the total current visitors and some key performance indicators about their activity on your website such as: • Total unique sessions visiting now • Total unique sessions visiting now from the top referrer • Total unique sessions watching videos now • Total unique sessions visiting now from the most popular device type • Total unique sessions visiting now and viewing content for the top interest

Universe Overview

Each star in the universe represents a group of users with the same interest from the same referrer. A legend at the bottom of the screen indicates the number of unique sessions within a single group / star. The top referrers are also shown in the legend and can be color-coded via the Live Dashboard Configuration Builder Module.

Auto-Zoom (aka ‘star callouts’)

Providing the user with a sensation of browsing the big data, individual stars are zoomed-in to explain how many unique sessions are currently visiting your site for the named top interest from the named top referrer.

Top Referrers

This scene is introduced with the question, ‘What are the most popular topics among [your website] visitors?’ The session groups / stars have been exploded into identically-sized stars representing a single user each and assembled into star clusters orbiting their topic of interest from among the top interests of all current visitors.

Top Video

This scene is introduce with the question, ‘What is the most popular video on [your website] now?’ The video with most views by the current visitors plays in its entirety. Video formats supported include .mp4 and YouTube (embedded).
