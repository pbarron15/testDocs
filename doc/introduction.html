Introduction

The Profile Live Dashboard helps you experience in real-time the big data collected by the SAP Hybris Profile regarding visitor activity on your website. Unlike any other dashboard, the Profile Live Dashboard app delivers sensory-pleasing animations to inform you of customer engagement levels, website interests, and traffic origins -- while real time counters rise and fall before your eyes.

SAP Hybris Profile creates a contextual and consistent customer profile which enables all front office applications to engage customers with relevancy and deliver exceptional customer experiences. It enriches the customer profile on a real time basis to update the dynamic relationship between customer, brands, products, services, as well as any other relevant objects in your business, to provide a live and continuously evolving profile of each customer. Now, with the Profile Live Dashboard, you can visualize these connections in real time, in a whole new way.

First availability is an closed Beta package on YaaS effective May 30, 2017.

Key Features

Feature Description
Current Visitor Count (real time)
Watch your website visitor counts rise and fall before your eyes in real time. The total count includes all unique sessions who have visited the site within one hour of the current time. For example, a visitor on the site in the last 5 seconds will be included in the count, a visitor on the site in the last 1 hr will be included in the count, but a visitor on the site in the last 1hr and 5 sec will no longer be included in the count.
Top Referrers (real time)
Learn who is referring current visitors to your site. The domain names of the top 5 websites which are currently referring visitors to your site are highlighted and exact session counts are included.
Top Interests (real time)
Learn what assets on your website the current visitors are interested in. The top 5 most popular asset tags are highlighted and exact session counts are included.
Top Video (real time)
Watch the video on your website which is most played by the current visitors. If no video assets exist on your site, this feature will not appear. Video formats supported include .mp4 and YouTube embedded URLs.
Story-Telling (real time)
Sit back and enjoy an animation sequence of sensory-pleasing visualizations which tell a captivating story about who is visiting your website right now, where are they coming from and what are they most interested in.
Auto Zoom (real time browsing)
Take a closer look at select data points as the story-telling animations highlight groups of users who share the same interests and have been referred to your site by the same domain name. Exact session counts are included.
Configuration
Tailor the visualizations to suit your needs. Options include: a. Website / Brand Name for display in visualizations b. Referrer Brand Name + Color for display in visualizations
Failover Support (replay)
The Profile Live Dashboard app delegates your data management to secure YaaS services dedicated to the safeguarding of proprietary information relevant to your website activity. In the unlikely event that one of these delegate services becomes temporarily unavailable, the Profile Live Dashboard will replay the last 10 minutes of your data from a secure cache and therefore will suppress the ‘[LIVE]’ label so as to keep the viewer’s experience delightful while preserving the integrity of the story being told.
